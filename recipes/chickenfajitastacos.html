Chicken Fajitas Tacos

Chicken Fajitas Tacos are a delightful fusion of sizzling grilled chicken strips, colorful bell peppers, and onions, all seasoned with a blend of smoky spices. Served on warm, soft tortillas, these tacos are topped with fresh cilantro, a squeeze of lime, and a dollop of creamy guacamole or sour cream. Each bite offers a perfect balance of juicy chicken, crisp vegetables, and bold flavors, making them a delicious and satisfying meal for any time of the day.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • Bell Peppers
  • Mushrooms

Steps

  1. Grill the chicken breast
  2. Chop bell peppers and mushrooms
  3. Grill the chicken for on medium heat for 5 minutes each side then add chopped bellpeppers and mushrooms
