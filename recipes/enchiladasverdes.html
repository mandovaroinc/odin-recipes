Enchiladas Verdes

Enchiladas Verdes are a traditional Mexican dish featuring soft corn tortillas filled with tender shredded chicken, then rolled up and smothered in a tangy and flavorful green tomatillo sauce. Topped with melted cheese, a sprinkle of fresh cilantro, and a dollop of creamy sour cream, these enchiladas are bursting with zesty flavors. Perfectly accompanied by rice and beans, this dish offers a delicious and satisfying meal that brings the vibrant tastes of Mexico to your table.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • Tortillas
  • Salsa

Steps

  1. Boil the chicken breast
  2. Prepare the salsa
  3. Once the chicken is fully cooked, use a fork to shred
  4. Line the tortillas on an oven pan, lather with salsa and add the shredded chicken
  5. Bake for 15 minutes at 350 degrees
