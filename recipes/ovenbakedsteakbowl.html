Oven Baked Steak Bowl

The Oven Baked Steak Bowl is a hearty and flavorful dish featuring tender steak bites, crispy roasted potatoes, and a medley of colorful vegetables. The steak bites are seasoned to perfection and baked until juicy and succulent. Golden-brown potatoes provide a satisfying crunch, while the roasted vegetables add a burst of vibrant colors and a rich, savory taste. This wholesome bowl is not only visually appealing but also a balanced meal that combines protein, carbohydrates, and essential nutrients. Perfect for a comforting dinner or a nutritious meal prep option, this steak bowl brings together the best of oven-roasted goodness in every bite.

Ingredients

  • Steak
  • Russet Potatoes
  • Sweet Potatoes
  • Asparagus
  • Other veggies like broccoli and mushrooms

Steps

  1. Prepare and chop steak bites
  2. Chop both russet and sweet potatoes as well as all other veggies
  3. Chop veggies and disperse all ingredients evenly into foil bowls
  4. Wrap foil around food and bake at 450 degrees for half an hour
